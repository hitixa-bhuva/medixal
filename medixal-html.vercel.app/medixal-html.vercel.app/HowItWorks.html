Faq’s

Feel Free Asked Any Questions

What is Easy Booking and who is it for?

Easy Booking is an online appointment booking system designed for service-based businesses like salons, clinics, fitness studios, consultants, and freelancers. It helps you manage appointments, accept payments, and stay organized — all from one easy-to-use platform.

Do I need a website to use Easy Booking?

No! You’ll get a personal booking link and branded booking page you can share via WhatsApp, social media, or email — no separate website required.

Can I accept payments online through this system?

Yes. You can accept online payments via UPI, debit/credit cards, and wallets securely at the time of booking.

How will I know when a customer books an appointment?

You’ll receive instant notifications via WhatsApp, email, and/or SMS whenever a customer makes, cancels, or reschedules a booking.
