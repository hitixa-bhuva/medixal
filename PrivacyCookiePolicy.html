1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal and business information:

a. From Businesses (Vendors)

  • Business name & address
  • Contact person's name, phone, and email
  • Service offerings, pricing, and working hours
  • Booking preferences and availability settings
  • Payment details (where applicable)

b. From Clients (End Users)

  • Name, phone number, email
  • Booking details (chosen service, time, staff)
  • Payment information (if using online payment)
  • Reviews and ratings (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To manage appointments and bookings
  • To send reminders via email, SMS, or WhatsApp
  • To process payments securely (via third-party payment gateways)
  • To personalize your booking experience
  • To provide customer support and service updates
  • To improve our product and features

3. Sharing of Information

We never sell or rent your data.

We only share data:

  • With third-party service providers (e.g., SMS/email services, payment gateways) — strictly for service delivery
  • With team members (in your vendor dashboard) who manage bookings
  • When legally required to comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and trackers to improve your experience, such as:

  • Remembering your login
  • Analyzing user behavior
  • Improving website performance
  • Tracking marketing performance (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel)

You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings or through our cookie consent banner.

5. Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure admin authentication
  • Limited access controls
  • Encrypted third-party payment gateways
  • Daily data backups
Important: However, no system is 100% secure. You use our platform at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws (e.g., GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing or data processing
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

You can request any of the above by contacting us at: privacy@easybooking.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As needed to provide services and fulfill our obligations
  • As required by law or for dispute resolution

Once no longer required, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Third-Party Services

Easy Booking integrates with trusted third-party platforms such as:

  • WhatsApp API for reminders
  • Razorpay/Stripe/UPI for payments
  • Google Calendar (optional sync)
  • Analytics tools like Google Analytics, Meta Pixel

These services may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that we have, we will delete it immediately.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in laws, technology, or our services. We encourage you to check this page regularly. If major changes occur, we will notify you via email or a site notice.
