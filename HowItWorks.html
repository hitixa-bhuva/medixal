Faq’s

Feel Free Asked Any Questions

Is there an app for customers and businesses?

Yes. We provide both a Client App (for your customers to book appointments) and an Admin App (for vendors to manage bookings, payments, and schedules on the go).

Can I customize the services, pricing, and time slots?

Absolutely. You have full control to set your services, pricing, durations, staff members, and available time slots from your Admin Panel.

What happens after the free trial ends?

After the 7-day free trial, you can choose from one of our affordable subscription plans. You won’t be charged unless you upgrade — and your data stays safe

How many bookings can I accept during the free trial?

You can accept up to 50 client bookings during your free trial — with full access to all features.
